About Me

I am an undergraduate student currently in the third year of a four-year B.Sc. degree in Computer Science with an Industrial Placement at Newcastle University. As part of my course I'm doing a year-long research placement in Dr. Lucy Collinson’s group in the Electron Microscopy Science Technology Platform at the Francis Crick Institute in London. Working under the supervision of Dr. Martin Jones, I am developing bioimage analysis plugins for napari that use machine learning techniques to automate the targeted analysis and segmentation of mitochondrial cristae in volume electron microscopy images.

Conference presentations

The Society of Electron Microscope Technology (SEMT) 2022: RMS Early Career Speakers' Competition

I had the pleasure of taking part in the RMS Early Career Speakers' Competition which took place at the amazing Natural History Museum in London on the 7th of December 2022. Here I presented my research on automating the targeted analysis of mitochondrial cristae in volume electron microscopy images using random forests.
