Matouš Elphick

PhD Student | The Francis Crick Institute, The Institute for Cancer Research, Imperial College London

I am a first-year Convergence Science PhD student funded by Cancer Research UK, based at the Francis Crick Institute and Imperial College London. Working under the guidance of Prof. Samra Turajilic and Dr. Guang Yang, my research focuses on developing artificial intelligence models to analyse haematoxylin and eosin (H&E) whole slide images from the TRACERx Renal cohort. By automating this analysis, my goal is to facilitate downstream investigations into the tumor microenvironment of renal cancer carcinoma.
