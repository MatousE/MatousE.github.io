CrAIC Digital Pathology Journal Club (DPJC)

Welcome to the CrAIC Digital Pathology Journal Club! Our club gathers at the intersection of artificial intelligence and digital pathology, fostering discussions around the latest research and innovations. Hosted by the Francis Crick Institute, we aim to bridge the gap between computational science and clinical applications, bringing together experts, researchers, and enthusiasts.

Join us for regular meetings, insightful discussions, and a collaborative environment where we explore new frontiers in AI and pathology.
